About Me

My name is Marium Akmal, and I am a first-year Computer Science undergraduate at Punjab University College of Information Technology (PUCIT). I am currently focused on learning front-end web development and understanding how websites are built from scratch.

Right now, I work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I also have a solid foundation in C programming. I enjoy experimenting with layouts, styling, and small interactions, and I am slowly building confidence in my coding skills.

I am especially interested in artificial intelligence and robotics, and I like reading and learning about how intelligent systems work. These interests motivate me to keep improving my technical skills.

Outside of coding, I enjoy baking and watching animated movies, which help me relax and stay creative.

My goal is to complete my degree, gain practical experience in tech, and later pursue a Masters degree. I hope to work on projects that combine web development and artificial intelligence in a meaningful way.
